PIRATES 5992 is a FIRST Robotics Team based in West Orange, New Jersey from Seton Hall Preparatory School. Our main goal is to follow the mission of FIRST by engaging our members in various STEM activites, encouraging students to innovate collectively and introducing them to a career in STEM.

Founded in 1856, Seton Hall Preparatory School is New Jersey's oldest Catholic prep school. More than 950 students travel each day from over 130 towns to experience a challenging and comprehensive program of academics, activities, athletics, spirituality and service.
