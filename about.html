Seton Hall Preparatory School’s motto is Hazard Zet Forward, which translates to “despite hazards, move forward.” Team 5992 Pirates embodies this motto in the construction of a robot, which competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Although setbacks may occur in designing, prototyping, fabricating, and operating the robot, the team strives to solve and overcome these problems by building the best possible robot. The example of the team promotes the ideals of FIRST and STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) within the school and in the community beyond.

At the beginning of each school year, we start our recruiting process by giving a demonstration of our robot in the gymnasium and allowing anyone interested about robotics a chance to drive and learn about its functions. We then hand out flyers, which give the time and place of our first meeting, around our school to promote the Robotics Team. This meeting gives an extensive overview of our team and what the robotics season entails. Students can then decide if they want to join. We also reach out to the alumni of our club to come and give our new recruits the guidance and advice they need to have a good season.


Based at Seton Hall Preparatory School, the Pirates began in 2016 with a small team of seven. After the team’s rookie year, we have more than tripled in size. In 2017 the team managed to make it to the playoffs as alliance captains at the Mt. Olive district event. In 2018, the team secured its first blue banner by winning the Montgomery district event.
