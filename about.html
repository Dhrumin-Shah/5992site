About

2015: Robotics team founded with small group of students and low funding.


2016: First competition, FIRST Stronghold. Competes at Bridgewater and Montgomery. Wins Rookie of the year award. Offseason begins. Club doubles in size, new mentor joins, relationships with more teams established, Robotics becomes more organized of an activity.


2017: Second competition, FIRST Steamworks. Competes at Mount Olive, becomes alliance leaders for first time, loses in quarterfinals. Competes at Bridgewater. Offseason begins. Team once again doubles in size. Many more younger members join. More members become more adverse in skills required of the members.


2018: Third competition, FIRST Powerup. More funding, experience, and organization leads to a highest quality robot in team’s existence. Competes at Bridgewater, comes in at Semifinal as a replacement robot but does not make it to finals. Competes at Montgomery and strong showing as a support robot leads to the Team being picked by top seeded alliance and winning their first district event. Team 5992 is invited to first regional event in team’s history. Offseason: more work is put into how to fundraise, gain sponsors, increase its outreach, and become an overall better, more experienced team. Students from Mount Saint Dominic Academy join the team as well.
